# Deep Dive: The Z₃ Group Structure

## What is Z₃?
The cyclic group Z₃ = {0, 1, 2} under addition modulo 3.
Our ternary system {-1, 0, +1} is isomorphic to Z₃ under the mapping:
- -1 → 2 (mod 3)
- 0 → 0 (mod 3)
- +1 → 1 (mod 3)

## Why this matters
Every ternary operation is a group action on Z₃ⁿ.
Rotation, inversion, retrograde, addition — all are group actions.

## Paper
Fiore & Satyendra (2005). "Generalized Contextual Groups." MTO 11.3.
This paper describes Z₃ groups for chord voicings — exactly our system.
